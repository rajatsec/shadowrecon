# ShadowRecon / @secure_with_rajat — Instagram Slide Style Guide

## Visual Identity

### Background
- Pure black (#000000) OR very dark navy blue (#050A14)
- Blue glowing cosmic/organic particles overlay — glowing flowers, tree branches, stars, floating light orbs
- Crescent moon in top corner (subtle)
- NO plain flat backgrounds — always dark + atmospheric

### Typography
- **Titles**: Ultra-bold, heavy weight (Impact / Bebas Neue style), ALL CAPS or Title Case
- **Slide numbers**: Bold prefix format → `1/`, `2/`, `3/` etc.
- **Body text**: Regular weight white, clean readable sans-serif
- **Accent text**: Cyan (#00FFFF) or red (#FF2222) for emphasis
- **Color**: White primary, cyan/red accents only

### Layout Rules
- Cover slide: Giant bold title (left-aligned or centered) + AI cyberpunk artwork on right OR full bleed
- Content slides: Slide number + title top-left, body text below, lots of breathing room
- Bottom of every slide: `@secure_with_rajat` watermark (small, centered)
- NO crowded layouts — max 5-6 bullet points per slide
- Portrait format: 9:16 (Instagram Story/Carousel ratio)

### Tone & Content Format
- Hook slide: One powerful sentence, centered, mysterious/dramatic
- Content slides: `X/ Bold Title` → 4-5 short bullet points
- Closing slide: "Save this & Share it" CTA or Follow CTA

### Color Palette
- Background: #000000 or #050A14
- Primary text: #FFFFFF
- Accent 1: #00B4FF (electric blue)
- Accent 2: #FF2222 (blood red for dramatic effect)
- Glow effects: #0066FF with blur/bloom

### PRIMARY STYLE (Red Grunge Horror — Main Style)
- **Background**: Pure black #000000
- **Edge texture**: Dark red blood splatter / paint grunge on ALL 4 edges (looks like digital corruption or blood spray)
- **Title font**: Giant bold italic distressed/grunge red font — takes up top 40-60% of slide
- **Body font**: Regular white sans-serif, bullet points with • dots
- **Cover**: Multicolor title (lime green + white + purple/violet lines) + AI dark creature/villain artwork left side
- **Accent color**: Bright red (#FF0000) for titles, lime green (#AAFF00) + purple (#CC44FF) for cover accents
- **Watermark**: @secure_with_rajat bottom center, small white
- **Mood**: Urgent, breaking news, horror cyberpunk, dramatic
- **Canva style keyword**: "pure black background dark red blood splatter grunge edges giant bold italic red distressed horror font white body text dramatic urgent cyberpunk breaking news"

### SECONDARY STYLE (Blue Cosmic — Alternative)
- **Background**: Very dark navy #050A14
- **Overlay**: Blue glowing bioluminescent particles, flowers, stars, crescent moon
- **Font**: Ultra bold white Impact/Bebas condensed
- **Mood**: Cinematic, mysterious, atmospheric

## Slide Structure Template (for any tool/project)

```
Slide 1 — COVER
- Giant bold title (tool/topic name)
- 1 line hook subtitle
- @secure_with_rajat

Slide 2 — HOOK / PROBLEM
- 1 powerful statement, centered
- "Save this & Share it" or similar hook

Slides 3-8 — CONTENT (numbered X/)
- X/ BOLD TITLE
- 4-5 short bullet points
- Technical but readable

Slide 9 — CTA / FOLLOW
- "Follow @secure_with_rajat"
- "Like + Save if this helped"
- GitHub or resource link
```
